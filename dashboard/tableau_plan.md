# Tableau Dashboard Plan

## Dashboard Title
Workforce Retention Risk Dashboard

## KPI Cards

1. Attrition Rate
2. Overwork Rate
3. Project Overload Rate
4. Promotion Gap Rate
5. High-Risk Employee Share
6. Retention Priority Score

## Suggested Sheets

| Sheet | Chart Type | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Attrition by Department | Bar chart | Compare departure patterns by department |
| Monthly Hours vs Satisfaction | Scatter plot | Identify overwork and low satisfaction clusters |
| Project Count vs Attrition | Stacked bar chart | Show workload burden and attrition pattern |
| Tenure Risk Profile | Bar or line chart | Show risk by tenure group |
| Risk Segment Distribution | Donut or bar chart | Summarize Low / Moderate / High / Critical risk |
| Retention Priority Matrix | Heatmap | Combine workload, tenure, and predicted risk |

## Design Direction

Use a white and light coffee theme consistent with the website:

- Background: warm white
- Primary accent: coffee brown
- Secondary accent: soft beige
- Highlight: muted orange-brown
- Font: clean sans-serif
- Layout: KPI row on top, risk visualizations in the middle, recommendation panel at bottom
