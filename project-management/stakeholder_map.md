# Stakeholder Map

| Stakeholder | Interest | Decision Need |
|---|---|---|
| HR Department | Reduce employee turnover and improve satisfaction | Identify risk factors and intervention priorities |
| Senior Management | Control hiring cost and stabilize workforce | Understand organizational risk and policy implications |
| Department Managers | Manage workload and team performance | Detect overwork, project overload, and promotion gaps |
| Employees | Fair treatment, reasonable workload, career development | Ensure the model is used to support, not punish |
| Data Analyst | Build valid, interpretable, and ethical analytics outputs | Communicate model limits and actionable insights |
